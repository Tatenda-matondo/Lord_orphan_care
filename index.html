    About Us

    Who we are

    Lord Orphan Care is a non-profit organization founded in 1999 to provide comprehensive care and support for orphans and vulnerable children in Kambuzuma. With a commitment to compassion and a holistic approach that includes education, emotional well-being, life skills development, and community integration, our organization aims to address these challenges.

    What we do

    Lord Orphan Care is a private voluntary organisation which provides shelter for orphans, and extends feeding and clothing to the less privileged children in the high density suburb of Kambuzuma. We also offer assistance in the provision of school fees as well as offering hands skills training. Lastly the Organisation advocates for awareness against HIV/Aids, alcoholism, child / sexual abuse, etc

    Ultimate Goal

    By providing a safe, nurturing environment and comprehensive care and services, we aim to transform the lives of orphaned and at-risk children, giving them the tools and opportunities to break the cycle of poverty, neglect, and despair. Our goal is to see these children grow into self-sufficient, thriving adults who can positively impact their communities and the world around them.

    Our Vision

    Lord Orphan Care was founded in 1999 with a vision for a world where every orphaned and vulnerable child is loved, nurtured, and empowered to reach their full potential. We envision a future where all children, regardless of their circumstances, have access to the basic necessities of life - food, shelter, education, and healthcare - as well as the support and guidance they need to develop into confident, compassionate, and productive members of society.

    Mission

    We envision a world where no child is left behind - where every child has a loving home, a quality education, and the chance to reach their full God-given potential. Through our work, we strive to be a beacon of hope, empowering vulnerable children to overcome adversity and create a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

    Core Values

    Lord Orphan Care is a Christian organization that supports orphans and vulnerable children. Our mission is based on Christian principles, rooted in the Bible's teachings. We believe in treating every child with dignity and respect, creating a nurturing environment where they feel valued and empowered. We engage with the local community through partnerships with schools, churches, and other organizations to provide resources and services.

    Current Projects

    At our organization, we have a diverse range of projects that we are actively working on. These projects span various domains and are designed to help train the children to address the unique needs and challenges they face.

    Fashion and Frabics

    Our fashion design program equips students with essential skills for the fashion industry, including dress-making, shoe-making, garment construction, design principles, and trend analysis.

    Poultry and Egg Farming

    Our agriculture program offers practical experience in sustainable farming techniques, and egg farming, as well as bicycle repairs to work hands-on an and apply their knowledge to real-world scenarios.

    Our Founding Members

    Lord Orphan Care was founded by a dedicated team of four individuals who recognized the need for comprehensive support and care for orphans and vulnerable children in Kambuzuma. These founding members have been the driving force bringing a diverse range of expertise and experience to the organization, allowing us to deliver comprehensive support and care.
